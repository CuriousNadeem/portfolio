Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at ShamsRig. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you place an order or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address, and payment confirmation screenshot.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process your order, communicate with you, and ensure smooth delivery. We may also use anonymized data for analytics and product improvement purposes.

3. Screenshot & Payment Proof

Payment screenshots are uploaded during checkout for manual order verification. These are securely stored and only accessible to authorized personnel. We do not share these with third parties.

4. Email Communication

We send confirmation emails after each order and status updates during processing. You may also receive service messages related to your order. We do not spam or sell your email.

5. Data Protection & Security

All information is stored securely. We use secure directories for screenshots and order data. Access is restricted and monitored for safety. We do not collect or store any credit card or banking credentials.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses basic cookies to enhance functionality and performance. We do not track your personal activity beyond our site or use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like EmailJS for email delivery. These services only receive necessary data to function and are GDPR-compliant.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update our privacy policy. If we do, we’ll post the update date clearly on this page. We recommend checking periodically.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, feel free to contact us through our website or email us at atshammughal28@gmail.com.
